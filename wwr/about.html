History

Founded in 1990 by the Johnson family, our whitewater rafting company emerged from a shared passion for adventure and a deep appreciation for the great outdoors. Nestled in the heart of stunning river valleys and surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, our family envisioned a place where adventure-seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and nature lovers could come together to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting.
